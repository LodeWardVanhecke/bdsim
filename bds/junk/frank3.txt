Hi Grahame,

to generate the distribution with 
nonzero alfas I use the following
lines of code:


      phix = ran2(idum)*2.*pi
      phiy = ran2(idum)*2.*pi
      ex = -dLog(RAN2(idum))*emx
      ey = -dLog(RAN2(idum))*emy
      X=  sqrt(2*ex*betx0)*sin(phix)
      PX= sqrt(2*ex/betx0)*(cos(phix)-alfx0*sin(phix))
      Y=  sqrt(2*eY*betY0)*sin(phiY)
      PY= sqrt(2*eY/betY0)*(cos(phiY)-alfY0*sin(phiY))
      T=  sigz*(ran2(idum)-0.5)
      DELTAP=(ran2(idum)-0.5)*Deltaw
